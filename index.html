Scruby Companion — Gameplay Instructions

Advanced MMORPG Companion Mod for Hytale

📺 For screenshots, trailer, and visuals, see the full mod page on CurseForge: https://www.curseforge.com/hytale/mods/scruby-companion

Quick Start

  1. Install the Scruby Companion mod and the HyUI dependency mod.
  2. Enable both in your game settings under Mods.
  3. Join your world. Your first Scruby will automatically spawn and start following you.
  4. Open the Scruby menu anytime with /scruby-menu or look at him and press F.

That's it — you're ready to play.

Useful Commands

  • /scruby-menu — open the main Scruby UI
  • /scruby — list all player commands and settings
  • /scruby-admin — server admin commands (op-mode required); use it to instantly level up and more

First Steps (Level 1–5)

Your Scruby starts at level 1 with no class.

  • Kill mobs together. Scruby earns XP from your kills and his own.
  • First kills on a new NPC type give 2× XP (Discovery System). Explore actively.
  • At level 5, the path selection unlocks. Choose between DPS, Healer, or Tank.

Choosing a Path (Level 5)

Open the Scruby menu and select your path:

  • DPS — high damage output, ranged abilities
  • Healer — keeps you alive with regeneration and healing skills
  • Tank — soaks up damage, draws aggro, high HP focus

Each path has its own skill set and visual evolution.

Combat Modes

Scruby has two combat modes you can switch anytime from the character tab:

  • Attack Mode — Scruby attacks every hostile NPC within 20 blocks
  • Guard Mode — Scruby only attacks the enemies you target

Skilling Your Scruby

  • You earn 2 attribute points per level (Vitality, Strength, Zeal)
  • At level 12 and level 18, you choose 1 skill each time
  • Total of 22 skills available across all paths

Equipping Armor

  • Open the Scruby menu and go to the Item Transfer tab
  • Drag any native Hytale armor onto Scruby
  • Each piece adds HP and renders visually on Scruby's model

Scruby Inventory and Auto-Loot

  • Scruby has a built-in 20-slot inventory
  • He automatically picks up items dropped by NPCs he helped kill
  • Transfer items to and from Scruby via the Item Transfer tab

Base Guarding

  • Open the Scruby menu and set Scruby to "stationed" mode at your base location
  • He will defend the area, kill enemies within range, and stockpile loot
  • Return any time to collect what he gathered

Evolution Stages

Scruby visually evolves at:

  • Level 5 (after path selection)
  • Level 13
  • Level 20 (Prestige evolution)

Endgame: The Prestige Fight

At max level (20), the Prestige Fight unlocks:

  • Open the Scruby menu and challenge the Prestige boss
  • The boss has 500 HP and deals 1 AOE damage per second
  • Win: unlock the Legendary Prestige Skin (a status symbol on multiplayer servers)
  • Lose: no second chance (server admins can grant a retry via /scruby-admin)
